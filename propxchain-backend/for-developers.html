Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PropXchain for property developers.

How does PropXchain integrate with our existing systems?

PropXchain is designed to integrate with your existing property management and sales systems through secure APIs. Our implementation team will work with your IT department to ensure seamless integration with minimal disruption to your operations.

What is the implementation timeframe?

Typical implementation takes 4-6 weeks from initial assessment to pilot launch. Full deployment across multiple sites can be completed within 3 months, depending on the size and complexity of your development portfolio.

How do buyers and solicitors access the system?

Buyers and solicitors receive secure access to their side of the transaction dashboard through a web-based portal. No special software is required, and the interface is intuitive and user-friendly. We provide training materials and support for all users.

What are the costs involved?

PropXchain operates on a subscription model with an implementation fee and per-transaction charges. The exact pricing depends on your transaction volume and specific requirements. Our pricing is structured to deliver clear ROI, with typical cost savings of 48% per transaction compared to conventional methods.

Is PropXchain compliant with UK property laws and regulations?

Yes, PropXchain is designed to work within existing UK legal frameworks for property transactions. Our system complies with all relevant regulations including Land Registry requirements, GDPR, and anti-money laundering legislation. We regularly update the system to reflect any regulatory changes.

What support is provided after implementation?

PropXchain provides comprehensive ongoing support including a dedicated account manager, technical support team, regular system updates, and continuous optimization based on performance data. We also offer additional training for new staff members as needed.

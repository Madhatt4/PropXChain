Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about PropChain for conveyancing solicitors.

Is PropChain compliant with SRA regulations?

Yes, PropChain is designed to comply with all Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA) requirements. The system maintains appropriate audit trails, ensures client confidentiality, and supports your firm's compliance obligations. We regularly update the system to reflect any regulatory changes.

How does PropChain handle client funds and financial transactions?

PropChain does not directly handle client funds. The system integrates with your existing client account management processes and provides secure verification of fund transfers. All financial transactions remain under your firm's control and comply with SRA accounts rules.

What training is provided for our legal team?

PropChain provides comprehensive training for all users, including online tutorials, live training sessions, and detailed documentation. We offer both initial onboarding training and ongoing support as needed. The system is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, minimizing the learning curve for your team.

How does PropChain ensure data security and confidentiality?

PropChain employs enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. All data is stored in compliance with GDPR requirements, and access controls ensure that information is only available to authorized parties. The blockchain verification adds an additional layer of security by creating immutable records of all transactions.

Can we use PropChain for all property transactions or only new builds?

PropChain is initially focused on new build properties as a controlled environment to demonstrate value. However, our development roadmap includes expansion to the resale market. By partnering with PropChain now, your firm will be well-positioned to handle both new build and resale transactions as the platform evolves.

How does PropChain handle Land Registry integration?

PropChain integrates with Land Registry systems for title verification and registration updates. The system works within existing Land Registry frameworks while providing enhanced verification through blockchain technology. As Land Registry continues its own digital transformation, PropChain is designed to evolve alongside these changes.

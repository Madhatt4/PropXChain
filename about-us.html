About PropXchain

We're transforming property transactions through blockchain technology, creating a faster, more transparent, and more efficient process for all stakeholders.

Our Story

PropXchain was founded in 2024 based on a simple but powerful insight: property transactions in the UK are unnecessarily slow, expensive, and stressful. With decades of combined experience in property development, legal services, and blockchain technology, our founding team set out to create a solution that would transform the property transaction process.

Based in North Wales, we're proud to be part of the UK's growing proptech ecosystem. Our location gives us access to both the vibrant Welsh innovation community and the broader UK property market, allowing us to develop and test our solution in a real-world environment.

After extensive research and development, we created PropXchain - a blockchain-based property transaction system that reduces completion times from 12-16 weeks to just 3-4 weeks while cutting direct costs by approximately 48%. Starting with new build properties as a controlled environment, we're building a scalable platform that will eventually transform the entire UK property market.

Our patent-pending technology has already attracted attention from forward-thinking property developers and conveyancing firms who share our vision for a more efficient, transparent property transaction process.

Our Mission

To reduce the time, cost, and stress of property transactions through blockchain technology, benefiting all stakeholders in the property ecosystem.

Our Vision

To transform property transactions into seamless, transparent, and efficient digital experiences, making property ownership more accessible and less stressful for everyone.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, collaboration, and excellence guide everything we do. We believe in creating technology that serves people, not the other way around.

Our Approach to Property Innovation

PropXchain's approach to innovation is guided by four key principles that ensure our technology delivers real-world benefits.

1

Work Within Existing Frameworks

We design our technology to work within existing legal and regulatory frameworks, ensuring practical implementation without requiring systemic change.

2

Focus on Practical Outcomes

We prioritize measurable improvements in transaction time, cost, and transparency over theoretical blockchain applications.

3

Collaborate with Industry Stakeholders

We work closely with property developers, solicitors, and other stakeholders to ensure our solution addresses real-world needs and challenges.

4

Iterate and Improve

We continuously refine our technology based on user feedback and performance data, ensuring ongoing improvement and adaptation.

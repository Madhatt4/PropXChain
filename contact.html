Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and working with PropXchain.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our office directly at +44 (0) 1234 567890.

What happens after I request a demo?

After requesting a demo, a member of our team will contact you to schedule a convenient time. We'll ask a few questions about your specific needs to tailor the demonstration accordingly. Demos typically last 45-60 minutes and can be conducted remotely or in person at your office.

Do you offer on-site presentations for our team?

Yes, we're happy to arrange on-site presentations for property development companies and conveyancing firms. These presentations can be customized to address your specific challenges and questions, and typically include a live demonstration of the PropXchain system.

How can we become an early adopter of PropXchain?

We're currently selecting a limited number of forward-thinking property developers and conveyancing firms to be early adopters of PropXchain. Early adopters receive preferential pricing, direct input into feature development, and dedicated implementation support. Contact us to discuss early adopter opportunities.

Where are you located?

PropXchain is headquartered in North Wales, UK. We serve clients throughout the UK and can arrange meetings at your location or ours. Many of our demonstrations and consultations can also be conducted remotely via video conference.
